Scam Awareness Tips

A few simple rules can help you stay safe from the most common scams.

Never share your OTP, PIN, or password with anyone, no matter who they claim to be.
Always verify unexpected job offers or lottery wins from official company websites or sources.
Be wary of links asking for payment or personal details. WhatsApp will never ask for money for its service.
Be cautious of unknown contacts claiming to be bank officials or government representatives. Always verify their identity through official channels.
